GENA Tokenomics

GENA (short for General Network Asset) is a general-purpose utility token designed to be used across various smart contracts and decentralized applications within the AISaaS ecosystem. Its core function is to support the suite of apps built or integrated into AISaaS, enabling features such as payments, service access, and internal utility transactions. GENA also serves as the primary token to support the operational and business continuity costs of the platform. It is not a governance token and does not provide public voting rights.
